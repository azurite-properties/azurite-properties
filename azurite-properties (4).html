Where Property Value Meets Professional Care

Azurite Properties Ltd is a proudly Kenyan real estate company headquartered in Mombasa and dedicated to the Coast. Whether you are a property owner in the diaspora seeking professional management, a first-time buyer navigating the coastal market, or a developer launching a landmark project — Azurite is the team that delivers.

Investment Opportunities

Why Invest in Kenyan Coastal Property?

The Kenya Coast offers a compelling mix of lifestyle value, rental yield, and long-term capital appreciation — making it one of East Africa's most attractive real estate investment destinations.

📈

Capital Appreciation

Coastal property values in Nyali, Diani, and Vipingo have consistently appreciated over the past decade, driven by infrastructure growth, tourism demand, and urbanisation. Early movers in emerging corridors like Likoni have seen strong gains.

Projected 5-Year Growth 35–60%
🏠

Strong Rental Yields

High demand for quality rental homes — from professionals, expats, NGO workers, and tourists — creates reliable rental income streams. Premium furnished apartments in Nyali and Kizingo command consistent occupancy at top-tier rents.

Typical Net Yield 7–12% p.a.
🌐

Off-Plan Opportunities

Early-stage investment in off-plan coastal developments offers the highest upside — acquisition at pre-launch prices, phased payment plans, and significant value creation by completion. Azurite maintains an active off-plan pipeline.

Typical Capital Uplift 20–45%
