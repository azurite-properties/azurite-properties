Buying & Investing

Buying Property on the Kenya Coast

Coastal property can offer both lifestyle value and rental income. Here is an honest overview of what buyers and investors should understand before committing.

📈

Capital Growth

Property values in well-located coastal areas — particularly Nyali, Diani, and Vipingo — have generally increased over the past decade. Growth is uneven across the Coast and depends heavily on location, property condition, and title type. We recommend independent legal due diligence on all purchases.

Our advice Always verify title, check for encumbrances, and use a licensed advocate. We can recommend local legal professionals.
🏠

Rental Income

Quality rental properties in Nyali, Kizingo, and Bamburi maintain consistent demand from professionals, expats, and corporate tenants. Short-let properties near the beach can earn significantly more per night but require active management. We can advise on realistic yield expectations for a specific property.

Rental yields vary Yields depend on location, furnishing level, and management quality. Ask us for a realistic estimate on a specific property.
🌐

Off-Plan Considerations

Off-plan purchases can offer early-investor pricing and structured payment plans. However, they carry completion and developer risk. We only market off-plan projects from developers we have assessed directly, and we are transparent about the risks involved in any project we present.

Do your due diligence We provide information on off-plan projects we market. Independent legal review is always recommended before committing.
