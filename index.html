Buying & Investing

Buying Property on the Kenya Coast

An honest overview of what buyers and investors should understand before committing.

📈

Capital Growth

Property values in well-located coastal areas — particularly Nyali, Diani, and Vipingo — have generally increased over the past decade. Growth is uneven and depends heavily on location, condition, and title type.

Our advice: Always verify title, check for encumbrances, and use a licensed advocate. We can recommend local legal professionals.
🏠

Rental Income

Quality rental properties in Nyali, Kizingo, and Bamburi maintain consistent demand from professionals, expats, and corporate tenants. We can advise on realistic yield expectations for a specific property.

Yields vary: Depends on location, furnishing level, and management quality. Ask us for a realistic estimate on any specific property.
🌐

Off-Plan Considerations

Off-plan purchases can offer early-investor pricing and structured payment plans. However, they carry completion and developer risk. We only market off-plan projects from developers we have assessed directly.

Do your due diligence: Independent legal review is always recommended before committing to any off-plan purchase.
