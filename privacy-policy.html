Azurite Properties Ltd — how we handle your personal information

Last updated:
This policy applies to information collected through our website and direct communications.

1. Who We Are

We can be contacted at: [email protected] or by phone at +254 117 000 666.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you:

  • Submit an enquiry through our website contact form
  • Contact us directly by phone, email, or WhatsApp
  • Enquire about a property listing
  • Enter into a service agreement with us (management, sales, or letting)

The information we may collect includes your name, phone number, email address, preferred location or budget, and the nature of your property enquiry.

We do not collect payment card details through this website. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes on this static site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your property enquiry
  • Match you with relevant properties or services
  • Follow up on an active instruction or tenancy
  • Send you property updates if you have asked to be kept informed

We will not use your contact details to send you unsolicited marketing, and we will not sell or share your information with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. How We Store Your Information

Enquiries submitted through our website form are sent directly to our email inbox. We do not operate a centralised database that stores website enquiries beyond our email account.

Information related to active client relationships (management agreements, tenancy records, transaction files) is stored securely in line with normal business record-keeping practices and applicable Kenyan law.

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent — for example, introducing you to a developer or seller
  • To fulfil a service — such as sharing contact details with a property owner when arranging a viewing
  • Legal obligation — if required by Kenyan law or a court order

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with marketing companies or data brokers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your information where we have no ongoing business reason to keep it
  • Opt out of any follow-up communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at [email protected].

7. WhatsApp Communications

If you contact us via WhatsApp, your messages are handled through WhatsApp's platform, which has its own terms and privacy policy (see whatsapp.com/legal). We use WhatsApp only for direct client communication and do not add you to group lists without your consent.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. We will not reduce your rights under this policy without providing appropriate notice.
