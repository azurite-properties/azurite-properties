About This Development

Ryan Heights is a premium off-plan residential development located on 5th Avenue Parklands, one of Nairobi's most prestigious and well-connected neighbourhoods. The development offers a range of apartment typologies — from compact 1-bedroom units to expansive 5-bedroom duplex apartments — each designed with refined interiors and elevated lifestyle amenities. Unit sizes range from 70 sqm to 285 sqm, with private balconies, modern open-plan living spaces, and high-specification bathroom and kitchen fittings throughout.
