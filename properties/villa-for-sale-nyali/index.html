About This Property

A rare opportunity to own a newly built contemporary villa in one of Nyali's most sought-after residential pockets. Set within a private shared compound of only two units — with one already sold — this is the last available unit. At 4,090 sq ft, the villa has been designed for refined family living, combining generous open spaces with high-specification finishes throughout.

The ground floor features a dramatic open-plan living and dining area with floor-to-ceiling glazing, a designer kitchen with a central island finished in granite and fluted wood panelling, built-in oven, and a full range of fitted cabinetry. The first floor houses four en-suite bedrooms, a dedicated children's living and games area, and a home office. The rooftop terrace offers private outdoor space with views over the neighbourhood. A detached en-suite DSQ provides additional accommodation for staff or guests.
